I, on own behalf and on behalf of my colleagues in the Electrical and
Electronics Engineering Department felicitate the Alumni Association for bringing
out the Souvenir of the outgoing batch of students of 1987.
This batch, more than the previous batches, has seen a stupendous progress
that the Institute has made during the past few years. There has been significant
all round improvements-on academic .as well on cultural fronts. The campus is
always vibrant. Students enjoy their studies and examinations as much as their
cultural events like Saturnalia, Ustavand SPIC-MACAY function. I am sure that
not only they would cherish the memories of their stay here and will remember them
with nostalgia but will be proud of being a product of a great up coming Institution.
. I wish the out going students a challenging career, which will bring the
best out of them and for which they have had ample training here.
A. K. SAWHNEY
Prof. & Head
Deptt. of Elect. & Electronics Engg.
