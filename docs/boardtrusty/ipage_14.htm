Dear Friends,
Souvenir-a7 has the special distinction of being the repository of
the rosy and dewey memories of the FIRST EVER BATCH of young engineers
passing out of the portals of this DEEMED UNIVERSITY. I am sure, the sense
of elation associated with the elevation of the status of your alma-mater
will infuse a new confidence in you. May this new cofidence embolden you
to face newer and grearer challanges in your real life in the DYBOSPHERE,
that this world has turned into.
Serious and sustained efforts have gone into the publication of this
document. I place on record my thanks to the members of the Souvenir
Committee; staff advisors as well as the student Commentators who have,
to my mind, succeeded in capturing the roving fantacies and floating clouds
and transformed them into this software package. We might doubly appreciate
this in the years to come.
While placing this Souvenir in yom hands, I welcome you all the
great fraternity of TI ET Alumni and hope for our bonds to strengthen. We
wish to be excused for imperfections in this publication which might have
crept in despite our care at all steps.
P. L. BALI
Secretary
Alumni Association
