Dear Engineers,
Now when you are about to step out from the portals of your Institute
to enter yet another era of your life, I wish you all the best.
No man, whosoever lived on this earth has escaped confrontation with the
most baleful, bitter facts of existence. It is within us not to "bleed" our hearts,
when we "fall upon the thorns of life". Afterall one can always rise to inhale the
fragrance of the rose smilling atop the thorny bush of life. Now when you are
standing at the bay of your young lives, let nothing and no one hamper your zest
to pursue your high aims. It would be sheer defeatism to opiate yourselves to
either inactivity or low aim. Try to expand your abilities instead of limiting them.
Meet new challenges and take advantage of opportunities the future holds for you
to shape your future and our country too.
India is passing through a period of of rapid scientific and technological
advancement and phenomenal socia-economic development. We are marching
towards 21 st century with a promise of hope and better quality of life for our
people. You are to steer the way. Be prepared to accept the quaintness of this
world and try to achieve the lofty aim with sincere hard work, truthfulness and also
always, essentially, with a smile.
My sincere prayers are always for you.
With expectations and warm wishes.
